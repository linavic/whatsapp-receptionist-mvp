# WhatsApp Receptionist - Product Blueprint

## Core Product

Digital receptionist for small businesses that use WhatsApp as their main sales and service channel.

The product answers incoming leads, guides them with buttons, books appointments, sends reminders, handles cancellations, offers freed slots to waitlisted customers, follows up with leads, and asks for Google reviews.

## One-Line Promise

We install an automatic WhatsApp receptionist that answers instantly, books appointments, reduces no-shows, and brings back missed leads.

## Best First Market

Beauty, wellness, and appointment-based clinics:

- Cosmetics clinics
- Pedicure and manicure
- Physiotherapy
- Massage therapists
- Hair salons
- Dental and private medical clinics
- Pilates and fitness studios
- Coaches and consultants

These businesses already feel the pain daily: missed WhatsApp messages, cancellations, no-shows, forgotten follow-ups, and manual scheduling.

## Buyer Pain

The owner is busy serving clients and cannot answer every message immediately. Leads disappear when they wait. Existing clients forget appointments. Cancellations create empty time slots. Follow-up is inconsistent.

## Why They Pay

The ROI is simple:

- One saved lead can cover the monthly fee.
- Two prevented no-shows can cover the monthly fee.
- One returning client generated by follow-up can cover the monthly fee.

## Adaptation Model

Every customer is configured through a small set of inputs:

- Business name
- Industry
- Services
- Staff members
- Working hours
- Booking rules
- Cancellation policy
- Reminder timing
- Payment/deposit policy
- Tone of voice
- Google review link
- Owner notification number

No custom code should be required for normal customer onboarding.

## Product Principle

Use buttons wherever possible. Avoid relying on free-text intent detection for critical actions like booking, cancelling, confirming, or payment.

Stable payload examples:

- `service_select:{service_id}`
- `appointment_request:{service_id}`
- `appointment_confirm:{appointment_id}`
- `appointment_cancel:{appointment_id}`
- `slot_reserve:{appointment_id}`
- `slot_decline:{appointment_id}`
- `lead_followup_yes:{lead_id}`
- `lead_followup_no:{lead_id}`

## Operational Promise

The owner should feel like they hired a reliable receptionist, not like they bought software.
