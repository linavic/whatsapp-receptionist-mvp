# Sales Playbook

## Positioning

Do not sell AI automation. Sell a digital receptionist that saves missed leads and prevents appointment losses.

## Opening Pitch

Most small businesses do not have a marketing problem first. They have a response-speed problem. Customers message on WhatsApp, wait, and then move to another business.

We install a WhatsApp receptionist that answers instantly, guides customers with buttons, books appointments, sends reminders, and brings back leads that did not close.

## Simple Offer

I can set this up for your business in a few days. You keep using WhatsApp. Customers get faster answers. You get fewer missed leads and fewer no-shows.

## Discovery Questions

- How many WhatsApp messages do you get per day?
- How fast do you usually answer new leads?
- How many appointments are cancelled or missed each week?
- Do you manually remind customers?
- Do you have a waiting list?
- Do you ask every customer for a Google review?
- If one more customer closed each week, what would that be worth?

## Demo Flow

1. Send a WhatsApp message to the demo number.
2. Show instant reply with buttons.
3. Click Book appointment.
4. Select service.
5. Reserve a slot.
6. Show owner notification.
7. Click cancel.
8. Show waitlist offer.
9. Show review request.

## Objection Handling

### "I already answer WhatsApp myself."

Great. This does not replace you. It protects you when you are busy, with a client, driving, or after hours.

### "I do not want a robot talking to customers."

The system uses clear buttons and your own wording. It feels like a professional receptionist, not a random chatbot.

### "Is this expensive?"

If it saves one lead or prevents a few no-shows, it usually pays for itself.

### "Can it fit my business?"

Yes. The structure is the same, but the services, wording, working hours, policies, and buttons are customized.
